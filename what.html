WHAT

Object work in the space implies the "what"

Scenes are about people's feelings/beliefs everything you "do" should flow from a belief or help you discover a belief

Avoid having things just "happening" in a scene. Actions should be meaningful

OBJECTIVES are things that you can take a series of concrete actions to achieve. AVOID THEM

DESIRES are things that you want but are unsure of how to get or are unable HAVE THEM

Gravitate toward INDEFINITE actions

Dont do a scene about getting the cap on the bottle. What happens when it is done

Instead do a scene where you are putting caps on bottles. You can choose to never run out. The scene wont be about the bottles.
